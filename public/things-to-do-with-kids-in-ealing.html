Outdoor activities for toddlers in Ealing

Ealing has a wealth of green spaces perfect for young children. Pitzhanger Park Play Centre in Walpole Park offers free play equipment for under 5s on weekdays. Brent Lodge Park (Hanwell Zoo) is a great low-cost outing with animals, a miniature train and acres of open space. Gunnersbury Park Nature Play area gives children of all ages the chance to explore natural outdoor environments.

Baby classes in Ealing for 0–12 months

Ealing has a thriving scene for baby classes. Hartbeeps is one of the most popular — their award-winning baby sensory and music classes run from Haven Green Church near Ealing Broadway. Many classes offer free taster sessions, so it's easy to try before committing to a term.

Frequently asked questions

What are the best free things to do with kids in Ealing?
Ealing has plenty of free options: Walpole Park has a great playground and the Pitzhanger Manor gardens. Gunnersbury Park has a nature play area, lake and miniature railway. Many local churches run free toddler groups — just bring a small donation if you can. LITTLElocals lists all free activities with a "Free" filter on the homepage.
What baby classes are available in Ealing?
Ealing has a wide range of baby classes including Hartbeeps (baby sensory and music), Sing and Sign (baby signing), Baby Sensory, and various toddler yoga and movement classes. Most run weekly term-time sessions and many offer free trial classes.
Are there activities for toddlers in Ealing on weekdays?
Yes — most of Ealing's baby and toddler classes run Monday to Friday. There are also daily-access soft play centres, parks with playgrounds, and toddler groups throughout the borough. LITTLElocals has a "Today" filter so you can instantly see what's on right now.
Is Hanwell Zoo good for toddlers?
Brent Lodge Park (Hanwell Zoo) is excellent for toddlers and young children. Entry is £5 per person and it has a small zoo with farm animals, a miniature train, and large open parkland. It's one of the most popular family spots in the Ealing borough and is open daily.
